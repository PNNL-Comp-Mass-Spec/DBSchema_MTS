This is a collection of DDL files that define the database schema used by
the Mass and Time Tag System (MTS) component of the Pan-Omics Research 
Information Storage and Management system (PRISM) at the Pacific Northwest 
National Laboratory (PNNL).  MTS is used to track and quantify peptides and
proteins identified in proteomics and related samples.
